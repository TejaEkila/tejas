Privacy Policy

At Tejas, we take your privacy seriously. We want to be completely transparent about how we handle your data.

Data Collection & Usage

We do NOT share any of your personal information with third-party services. Your data, including your goals, tasks, and habits, belongs solely to you.

Secure Cloud Storage

Your data is securely stored in the cloud using secure cloud infrastructure. This ensures your data is safe, backed up, and accessible across your devices, while remaining strictly private.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Our business model is built on providing value to you, not capitalizing on your data.

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you may request deletion directly within the app.

Process: When you request deletion, we will proceed with permanently removing your data within 48 hours. If you change your mind, simply sign in to Tejas again during this period to cancel the request.
